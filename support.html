How do I earn a stamp?

Open the Stadiums tab, find the venue you're at, and tap "I'm here — stamp my passport." You can add a photo and a short note before you confirm — that becomes part of your permanent stamp.

How do I share a stamp?

Open any stamp from your passport, then tap "Share this stamp" to save or send it as an image through Messages, Instagram, or anywhere else you'd normally share a photo.

I'm having trouble signing in
