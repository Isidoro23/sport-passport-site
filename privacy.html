SPORT PASSPORT Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 15, 2026

Sport Passport ("the app," "we," "us") lets you check in at pro sports stadiums and collect digital stamps. This page explains what information we collect, why, and how you can control it.

Information we collect

  • Account information: the email address and password you sign up with (handled by Firebase Authentication — we never see or store your password directly), plus the username and display name you choose.
  • Stamp data: which stadiums you check into, the date, and any note you write.
  • Photos: if you choose to add a photo to a stamp, that photo is uploaded and stored.

We don't collect precise location, contacts, or browsing data, and we don't use advertising trackers.

How your information is used

  • To create and secure your account
  • To save and display your passport and stamps
  • To let you share a stamp with other people, if you choose to

What's visible to other people

Stamps and profile display names are readable by anyone using the app — this is what makes sharing your passport with friends and family possible. Your email address and password are never shown to other users.

Where your data is stored

We use Firebase, Google's mobile app platform (built on Google Cloud), for authentication, database storage, and photo storage. Google's own privacy and security practices apply to how they host this data on our behalf — see Google's Privacy Policy. We don't sell your data to third parties or share it with advertisers.

Your choices

  • You can edit your profile information at any time from within the app.
  • You can request deletion of your account and all associated data by contacting us at the email below.

Children's privacy

Sport Passport is not directed at children under 13, and we don't knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child has created an account, contact us and we'll remove it.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we'll update the date at the top of this page.
